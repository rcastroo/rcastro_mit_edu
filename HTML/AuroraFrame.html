Aurora Frame
Links
Summary
This is a frame for an image rastered on acrylic. There is blue red and green LEDs hidden inside of an 1x1 aluminum tube which serves as a base for the acrylic which when turned on, shine light on the rastered acrylic, which gives the image look like an "aurora". It is a really cool frame that I plan on making much bigger and better over the next coming months. For now, I made two of the medium sized versions to give to two people I worked with at the MIT Edgerton Center.
Insides
In the inside of the aurora frame, there is 3 pieces of 1/8 wood, the middle piece being shorter than the others and all of them being the full length of the 1x1 aluminum. On that middle piece, The LEDs rest, and the acrylic rests on top of the LEDs. There is a switch on the back along with a battery pack. Hiding inside as well is the rest of the circuit: just a bunch of resistors and wiring.
Next
I plan on making a bigger version of this that can be wall mounted and be plugged into a wall outlet. In this way, you can put much bigger acrylic images on the frame and hang it up, which would look fantastic!
