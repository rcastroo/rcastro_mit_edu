Summary
I needed a way to carry my umbrella. See, my backpack only has 1 pocket, and it is already occupied by my water bottle, which is attached by a string to my backpack because I kept losing it. So on the opposite side of the water bottle, I designed, printed, and attached this 3D printed "slot" which can hold an umbrella easily! Additionally, I have a carabiner that I can attach to one of the holes to attach other things.

The slot is attached to the backpack via 2 bolts with washers on the other ends and lock nuts in order to prevent fraying. It is ridiculously useful!
